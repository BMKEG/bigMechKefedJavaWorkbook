Systems Biology KEfED Systems

The 'Knowledge Engineering from Experimental Design' (KEfED) model is a design approach for scientific knowledge representation based on focussing primarily on the variables that are set and observed within an experiment. Within this project, we specfically model the data structure based 'observables' within systems biology studies, which typically consist of many, small experiments, and our work is based on the idea of developing a library of experimental motifs that may be composed together to represent the scientific observations generated within a systems biology study.

This project is funded by the DARPA Big Mechanism program.
